"Winter"-The Days Of Endless Pain...

- 11TH JUNE,2020 -

Frosty, frozen

Glacial and glistening.

Cold-heart , Insomniac eyes

And the pain unending.


Weary, watery eyes

Tears on cheeks - freezing,

In a corner of bed,

I was shivering!


Slippery , snowy

The memories were depressing.

WINTER came for sure

T' was enormously isolating.


Bizzare, broken

Remembering the smile of yours

Legs gettin numb,

No bare - feeling!


Dusk, darkness

Were even more frightening!

Chills engrossed me up,

I was starving.


Feverish , frailing

People coming and leaving!

Getting persuaded for someone new,

(I wont ever...)

My summer,

swept away my everything

While leaving!


Drowsy, disordered

A worthless striving?

Looking at old clicks,

"Come back O Summer" to myself

Was I consistently crying and shouting?


Obnoxious, overshadowed

In my despair,

At times- Choking

I doubt, I doubt.

Will I see the next spring?


Sedentary , silently

Repeatedly, I was sobbing..

" Revive me - O Summer! "

Without you ever,

This WINTER would never be ending.





--To Anyone who has once lost someone they loved and wanted them back in their life , they might have felt this winter. You might have a question why it was not autumn? Because Autumn may describe it better - "tree shedding leaves and more!" I ll just say for me it was Winter-cold,bitter and harsh that left me Numb! For others it may feel autumn.


"The warmth of love is summer",its absence will always be -"The Winter"-Saurabh Chaubey
